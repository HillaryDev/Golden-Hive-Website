Our Story

Three Generations of Beekeeping Excellence

Founded in 2026 by Hillary, Golden Hive began with a single hive in a sunflower field in rural Nakuru. Today we tend over 800 hives across four pristine apiaries.

We believe honey should be as nature intended — raw, alive, and bursting with the flavors of the landscape where our bees forage.

🌍 Sustainably Sourced 🔬 Lab Tested 🏆 Award Winning 🌱 Regenerative Farms
How It's Made

From Hive to Table

Every step is handled with meticulous care to preserve the natural goodness of our honey.

🌸
1

Bees Forage

Our bees roam freely across pesticide-free wildflower meadows, collecting nectar from hundreds of flower species.

🍯
2

Natural Ripening

Bees transform nectar in the hive over 2–3 weeks, reducing moisture and developing complex flavor profiles.

⚙️
3

Cold Extraction

Honey is extracted without heat, preserving enzymes, antioxidants, and beneficial compounds in every jar.

📦
4

Packed Fresh

Each batch is tested, hand-labeled, and shipped within days of extraction to your door.
